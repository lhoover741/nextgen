License status cards

  • Valid driver licenses are required for civilian traffic compliance.
  • Suspended licenses may be enforced in police stops and DMV audits.
  • Expired registrations are flagged in vehicle lookups.
  • Insurance status is essential for road-based RP and incident handling.

DMV rules / notices

  • Drivers must follow RP traffic laws and yield to police signals.
  • Plate numbers should match official DMV registration records.
  • Suspended drivers can be cited or arrested depending on scene context.
  • Insurance status may affect traffic stop outcomes and RP consequences.
  • DMV fraud or false reporting can lead to warrants or staff review.
