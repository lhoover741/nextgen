Police rules

  • First police interaction must be obeyed.
  • Officers must control scenes professionally.
  • No abuse of police power or arbitrary enforcement.
  • Police must use proper RP procedure for stops, arrests, and detentions.
  • Arrests should be documented in CAD and evidence logged.
  • Evidence should be placed in police evidence lock-up.
  • Complaints against officers go through complaint-ticket channels.
  • Killing cops is not allowed unless approved by host/owner.
