Server conduct guide

Official NThaCityRP Rules

These rules keep Los Santos RP sharp, fair, and immersive for every member of the Discord community.

General RP rules

  • Stay in character during all roleplay interactions.
  • No fail RP. Actions must be realistic and justified in context.
  • No RDM. Do not attack or kill players without valid RP reason.
  • No VDM. Vehicle deaths must follow RP scenario and consent.
  • No metagaming. Keep Discord and OOC knowledge separate from IC actions.
  • No powergaming. Avoid actions that remove agency from other players.

Character rules

  • Characters must have a believable background and motivation.
  • Do not use the same name as an existing public staff or developer.
  • Keep roleplay names appropriate for Los Santos street culture.
  • Respect NPC use and server-managed roleplay scenes.

Police interaction rules

  • First police interaction must be obeyed unless it violates safety or rules.
  • Police cannot be killed unless approved by host/owner.
  • Failing to comply with police RP must still follow proper procedure.
  • Arrests must be documented in CAD and evidence preserved.
  • Complaints against officers go through the complaint-ticket system.

Vehicle rules

  • Follow traffic RP and stop when signaled by law enforcement.
  • Plate checks, registrations, and insurance must be roleplayed accurately.
  • Unregistered or stolen vehicles should be handled through the DMV or police channels.
  • Do not use vehicles to escape scenes without valid RP escalation.

Combat rules

  • Engage in combat only with appropriate RP justification.
  • Use safe zones and host-approved scenes for high-risk conflict.
  • No spawn camping or attacking players who are not actively engaged.
  • Respect wounds, arrests, and legal outcomes after combat.

Discord rules

  • Be respectful to staff and other community members.
  • Do not harass, threaten, or insult other players outside RP.
  • Keep obtrusive text, spoilers, and private discussions in proper channels.
  • Follow direction from moderators and report rule issues promptly.

Staff enforcement notice

Staff decisions are final where rule enforcement is concerned. Appeals and complaints must follow ticket procedure, and staff are empowered to protect RP integrity on the server.
