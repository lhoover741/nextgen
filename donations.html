Support NThaCityRP

Help keep the Discord community, server tools, graphics, and events running strong with safe and compliant support tiers.

Support overview

Donations support the community infrastructure and do not grant gameplay advantages, staff favoritism, or official RP immunity.

What support helps with

  • Server tools, bots, and community automation.
  • Graphics, brand assets, and event promotion.
  • Maintenance, hosting costs, and roleplay event coordination.
  • Community support and public recognition where allowed.

Support tiers

Street Supporter

Community recognition in Discord and a thank-you message from staff.

City Sponsor

Priority access to event updates and support for city-wide RP systems.

Elite Backer

Special cosmetic acknowledgment and support for long-term server maintenance.

Compliance notice

Support is voluntary and purely community-driven. Donations do not change staff decisions, server rules, or in-character enforcement.

Contact staff first

Always confirm support details with staff before sending funds. Unauthorized offers and outside agreements are not accepted.

Safe support language

Support is for community tools, maintenance, and events. It is not a purchase of game content, access, or roleplay power.
